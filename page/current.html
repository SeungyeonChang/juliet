A screen capture of the timer that Samsung phones come with
pre-installed is seen above. Although functional, it might be improved.
It is made to resemble a scroll wheel that you may use to select
a number between 0 and 60. Its lack of precision is the primary problem.
It is incredibly simple to scroll to the incorrect number or inadvertently alter a value. Having to navigate through all those numbers is also a little frustrating.
